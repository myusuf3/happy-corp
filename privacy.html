privacy, in plain words

We want to know if folks are visiting and coming back, and that’s it. Nothing more.

what we collect

  • Which page you visited.
  • How you got here (the site that referred you, if any).
  • Roughly where in the world you are (country-level, from your IP).
  • Your browser and device family (e.g. “Safari on iPhone”).

what we don’t collect

  • No cookies. Nothing stored in your browser by us.
  • No accounts, names, emails, or any other personal data.
  • No cross-site tracking. No advertising trackers. No fingerprinting.
  • Your IP address is used to compute the country and then immediately discarded.

how

We use Cloudflare Web Analytics, a privacy-first measurement tool that does not use cookies or fingerprinting and aggregates everything in a single dashboard. We don’t share the data with anyone.

questions

If something here doesn’t sit right with you, write to us at hello@aigrowthlabs.com and we’ll make it right.
